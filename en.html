Coming soon: the Swiss publising platform for open research data

Project Open Research Data

The pilot project Open Research Data (ORD@CH Pilot) will build, over a period of 18 months, a publishing platform for research data from different scientific areas in Switzerland. The core of the platform is a metadata catalogue of the databases of the participating institutions (FORS, the Digital Humanities Lab at the University of Basel, ETH Scientific IT Services / SIB Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics). The search function of ORD@CH allows all interested parties to find data beyond the boundaries of individual research institutions and projects, to access them and to use them further.

Goals of the ORD@CH Pilot

The modular design of the infrastructure will make it possible to provide access to the databases of other institutions gradually and integrate their metadata in the central catalog. The platform will continue to operate without major expense and be developed into a comprehensive metadata hub for all the research data of Switzerland after completion of the pilot project.
