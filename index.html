What is a CMS

CMS stands for Content Management System, a computer program that helps manage websites in general and specific aspects of sites, like blogs, news feeds, shopping features, etc. CMS may serve as a central archive for documents, movies, pictures, data, etc. A CMS makes it easy to publish, edit, and otherwise modify site content.

WordPress

WordPress is a commercial CMS product built on an Open Source foundation, thereby offering the best of both worlds. As a commercial product, WordPress is supported by a team of committed developers and technical support specialists. In our competent hands, WordPress becomes the technical foundation for a wide array of possibilities
